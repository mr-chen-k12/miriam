3.
The space shuttle orbited 155 miles above the earth. The weather balloon floated 15 miles above the Earth. The space shuttle was much higher than the weather balloon? Explain why your answer is reasonable.

To find the difference, we subtract the balloon's height from the shuttle's height.

$$155 - 15 = 140$$

Answer: 140 miles

Reasoning: The answer is reasonable because 155 is much larger than 15. The space shuttle operates in space (Low Earth Orbit), while weather balloons stay within the atmosphere.

26.
Which of the following segments is not a radius of a circle?
A. $$\overline{RS}$$
B. $$\overline{RM}$$
C. $$\overline{MT}$$
D. $$\overline{MS}$$

Definition: A radius is a line segment connecting the center (Point M) to any point on the edge of the circle.

  • RM: Connects center M to edge R. (Radius)
  • MT: Connects center M to edge T. (Radius)
  • MS: Connects center M to edge S. (Radius)
  • RS: Connects edge R to edge S, but does not start at the center. This is a chord.

Answer: A ($$\overline{RS}$$)

29.
In Dodge City, Kansas, the average maximum temperature in July is 93 degrees Fahrenheit. The average minimum temperature is 67 degrees Fahrenheit. How many degrees warmer is a temperature of 93 degrees Fahrenheit than a temperature of 67 degrees Fahrenheit?

Subtract the minimum from the maximum.

$$93 - 67 = 26$$

Answer: 26 degrees

The population density of Connecticut is 702.9 people per square mile. The population density of Kentucky is 101.7 people per square mile. Round to the nearest hundred to estimate how many more people per square mile live in Connecticut than live in Kentucky.

Step 1: Round to the nearest hundred.
702.9 rounds to 700.
101.7 rounds to 100.

Step 2: Estimate the difference.
$$700 - 100 = 600$$

Answer: Approximately 600 people per square mile
