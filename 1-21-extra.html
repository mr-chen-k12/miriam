Practice for Question 1 (Word Equations)

1-A
A warehouse had 350 boxes of apples. A second shipment arrived, and now the warehouse has 820 boxes total. Write an equation and solve to find how many boxes were in the second shipment.

Equation: \( 350 + x = 820 \) or \( 820 - 350 = x \)

Solution: \( 820 - 350 = 470 \)

Answer: 470 boxes

1-B
Julie read 145 pages on Monday. By Tuesday night, she had read a total of 315 pages. How many pages did she read on Tuesday?

Equation: \( 145 + p = 315 \)

Solution: \( 315 - 145 = 170 \)

Answer: 170 pages

7-A
The clock below shows an afternoon time. What time was it 12 hours before this time?

The clock shows 4:00.

Since it is afternoon, it is 4:00 PM.

12 hours prior to a PM time is the same time in AM.

Answer: 4:00 AM

7-B
The clock below shows a time in the morning. What time will it be 12 hours *after* this time?

The clock shows 9:30.

Since it is morning, it is 9:30 AM.

12 hours after AM is PM.

Answer: 9:30 PM

Practice for Question 6 (Sums & Roots)

6-A
The first four odd numbers are 1, 3, 5, and 7.
a. What is their sum?
b. What is the square root of their sum?

a. \( 1 + 3 + 5 + 7 = 16 \)

b. The square root of 16 is the number that multiplied by itself equals 16.

\( 4 \times 4 = 16 \)

Answer: 4

Sum: \( 13 + 11 + 12 = 36 \)

Square root: \( \sqrt{36} = 6 \)

Practice for Question 10 (Estimation)

10-A
Tom took 5 dozen big steps. If one big step is about 1 meter, about how many meters did he walk?

5 dozen = \( 5 \times 12 = 60 \) steps.

\( 60 \text{ steps} \times 1 \text{ meter} = 60 \text{ meters} \).

Answer: 60 meters

10-B
A car is about 4 meters long. If you line up 8 cars, about how many meters long is the line?

\( 8 \text{ cars} \times 4 \text{ meters} = 32 \text{ meters} \).

Answer: 32 meters

Practice for Question 27 (Circle Dimensions)

27-A
The distance from the center of a round pool to the edge is 9 feet. What is this distance called, and how many yards is it?

The distance from center to edge is the radius.

To convert feet to yards, divide by 3: \( 9 \div 3 = 3 \).

Answer: Radius; 3 yards.

27-B
The diameter (distance all the way across) of a circle is 10 meters. What is the radius?

The radius is exactly half of the diameter.

\( 10 \div 2 = 5 \).

Answer: 5 meters
