Question 1
Write and solve an equation: A bakery sold 340 muffins in the morning. By the end of the day, they had sold a total of 615 muffins. How many muffins were sold in the afternoon?

Equation: \( 340 + x = 615 \) or \( 615 - 340 = x \)

Subtract morning sales from total sales:

\( 615 - 340 = 275 \)

Answer: 275 muffins

Question 2
A pilot flew 125 miles on Friday, 140 miles on Saturday, and 115 miles on Sunday. How many miles did the pilot fly in total?

Sum the three distances:

\( 125 + 140 + 115 = x \)

\( 125 + 115 = 240 \)

\( 240 + 140 = 380 \)

Answer: 380 miles

Question 3
The decimal number four and sixty-five hundredths is how much more than one and twenty-five hundredths?

Convert to numbers:

  • Four and sixty-five hundredths = \( 4.65 \)
  • One and twenty-five hundredths = \( 1.25 \)

Subtract: \( 4.65 - 1.25 = 3.40 \)

Answer: 3.40 (Three and forty hundredths)

Question 4

b. Round 537 to the nearest ten.

a. Nearest Hundred:
Check the tens digit (3). It is less than 5, so round down.
Answer: 500

b. Nearest Ten:

Question 7
The clock shows a time in the evening (PM). What time was it 12 hours before?

The clock shows **8:00**. Since it is evening, it is **8:00 PM**.

12 hours before 8:00 PM is 8:00 AM.

Answer: 8:00 AM

Question 8

The clock shows 5:00. The angle spans 5 hour marks (150 degrees).

This is greater than 90 but less than 180.

Answer: Obtuse Angle

Question 9
a. Draw a square labeled ABCD.
b. Which side is parallel to side AD?

In a standard square labeling (clockwise starting top-left):

  • AD is the left vertical side.
  • BC is the right vertical side.

Vertical lines are parallel to each other.

Answer: Side BC
