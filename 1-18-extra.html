4.
How much change should you get back if you give the clerk $5.00 for a box of cereal that costs $3.85?

We need to calculate $$5.00 - 3.85$$.

Step-by-Step Double Borrowing:

  4910
  5.00
- 3.8 5
-------
  1.1 5

1. Start at the right (hundredths): $$0 - 5$$? We can't do it.
2. Look at the neighbor (tenths): It's also 0! We can't borrow from 0.
3. Go to the Ones place: Borrow from 5.
→ The 5 becomes 4.
→ The neighbor 0 becomes 10... but we need to pass 1 on.
→ So that 10 becomes 9.
→ Finally, the last 0 becomes 10.
4. Subtract:
$$10 - 5 = 5$$
$$9 - 8 = 1$$
$$4 - 3 = 1$$

Answer: $1.15

7.
The clock shows the time Joe left for work (7:10). He ate breakfast 35 minutes before that. What time did he eat?

Current Time: 7:10
Subtract: 35 minutes

You cannot subtract 35 from 10. We must "borrow" 1 hour from the 7.

  6  70 (10 + 60)
  7 : 10
- 0 : 35
--------
  6 : 35

1. Take 1 hour from 7, so it becomes 6.
2. Convert that hour to 60 minutes and add it to the 10 minutes.
3. $$60 + 10 = 70$$ minutes.
4. Now subtract: $$70 - 35 = 35$$.

Answer: 6:35

26.
Which segment is not a radius? ($$\overline{RS}$$, $$\overline{RM}$$, $$\overline{MT}$$, or $$\overline{MS}$$?)

A radius must connect the Center (M) to the edge.

  • RM, MT, and MS all touch the center.
  • RS connects two points on the edge without touching the center. It is a chord.

Answer: A ($$\overline{RS}$$)

29.
Temperature difference: Max 93°F, Min 67°F. How many degrees warmer is 93 than 67?

Guiding Questions:

  • When the question asks "How many more" or "How much warmer", what math operation should we use? (Addition or Subtraction?)
  • Set up the vertical math problem: 93 on top, 67 on bottom.
  • Look at the ones column: Can you do $$3 - 7$$? If not, what must you borrow from the 9?

$$93 - 67 = 26$$

Answer: 26 degrees

30.

Guiding Questions:

  • Rounding 702.9: Look at the hundreds place (7). Look at the neighbor (0). Do we stay at 700 or go up to 800?
  • Rounding 101.7: Look at the hundreds place (1). Look at the neighbor (0). Do we stay at 100 or go up to 200?
  • Once you have your two rounded numbers, subtract them to find the "estimate".

1. 702.9 rounds to 700.

2. 101.7 rounds to 100.

3. $$700 - 100 = 600$$.

Answer: 600 people per square mile.
