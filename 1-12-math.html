2. Takeshi had a dime, a quarter, and a penny. Write this amount using a dollar sign.
\(10\cent + 25\cent + 1\cent = 36\cent\)
Answer: \(\$0.36\)
3. Donna opened a 1-gallon container of milk and poured 1 quart out. How many quarts left?
1 gallon = 4 quarts.
\(4 - 1 = 3\).
Answer: 3 quarts
4. Find the next three numbers: ..., 4200, 4300, 4400, ___, ___, ___
Answer: 4500, 4600, 4700
6. Ananado fell asleep at the time shown (10:10). His alarm rings 8 hours later. What time is the alarm?
Clock shows 10:10.
\(10:10 + 8 \text{ hours} = 18:10\) (military) or 6:10.
7. Find the missing factor: \(5w = 45\)
Answer: \(w = 9\)

Answer: 1,050,000
26. If the area of a square is 36 square inches, how long is each side?
Area = Side \(\times\) Side.
\(6 \times 6 = 36\).
Answer: 6 inches.
