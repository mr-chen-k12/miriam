There are 120 marbles in a jar. They are separated into two bags. One bag has 45 marbles. How many marbles are in the other bag?
(Write and solve an equation)
Answer: 75 marbles

Equation: \( 120 - 45 = n \) or \( 45 + n = 120 \)

Solution: \( 120 - 45 = 75 \)

A pitcher held 2.5 liters of lemonade. Sarah poured out 1.2 liters. How many liters were left in the pitcher?
Answer: 1.3 liters

New York is 2,900 miles from Los Angeles. Chicago is 2,000 miles from Los Angeles (in roughly the same direction). If a plane flies from LA to Chicago, how many more miles must it fly to reach New York?
Answer: 900 miles

Subtract the distance already traveled from the total: \( 2900 - 2000 = 900 \).

29
A carpenter joins a board that is \(1\frac{1}{2}\) feet long with a board that is \(2\frac{1}{2}\) feet long. What is the total length?
Answer: 4 feet

\( 1 + 2 = 3 \) and \( \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{2} = 1 \).
Total: \( 3 + 1 = 4 \).

Swimmer A finished in 50.2 seconds. Swimmer B was 1.5 seconds slower. What was Swimmer B's time?
Answer: 51.7 seconds

"Slower" means more time.
\( 50.2 + 1.5 = 51.7 \).
