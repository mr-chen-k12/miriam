Question 1
A stadium has 450 seats in the lower section. The total capacity of the stadium is 920 seats. Write and solve an equation to find how many seats are in the upper section.

Equation: \( 450 + s = 920 \) or \( 920 - 450 = s \)

\( 920 - 450 = 470 \)

Answer: 470 seats

Question 2
A runner ran 12 miles on Monday, 15 miles on Wednesday, and 18 miles on Friday. What was the total distance run?

\( 12 + 15 + 18 = x \)

\( 12 + 18 = 30 \)

\( 30 + 15 = 45 \)

Answer: 45 miles

Question 3
The decimal number six and eighty-two hundredths is how much more than three and forty-one hundredths?

Convert to numbers: \( 6.82 \) and \( 3.41 \).

Subtract: \( 6.82 - 3.41 = 3.41 \)

Answer: 3.41 (Three and forty-one hundredths)

Question 4
a. Round 862 to the nearest hundred.
b. Round 862 to the nearest ten.

a. Nearest Hundred:
The tens digit is 6 (5 or more), so round up. 800 becomes 900.
Answer: 900

b. Nearest Ten:
The ones digit is 2 (less than 5), so round down. 62 becomes 60.
Answer: 860

Question 6
The first four odd numbers are 1, 3, 5, and 7.
a. What is their sum?
b. What is the square root of their sum?

a. Sum: \( 1 + 3 + 5 + 7 = 16 \)

b. Square Root: \( \sqrt{16} = 4 \)

Answer: 16 and 4

Question 7
The clock shows a time in the afternoon (PM). What time was it 12 hours before?

The clock shows **2:00**. Since it is afternoon, it is **2:00 PM**.

12 hours before 2:00 PM is 2:00 AM.

Answer: 2:00 AM

Question 8

Question 9
In a rectangle labeled WXYZ, which side is parallel to side WX?

Answer: Side YZ
