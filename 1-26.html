Question 1
Write and solve an equation:
A local library has 2,415 fiction books and 1,890 non-fiction books. What is the total number of books?
Combine the two amounts: $$ 2415 + 1890 = 4305 $$
Answer: 4,305 books
Question 2
A driver traveled 120 miles on Friday, 95 miles on Saturday, and 140 miles on Sunday. How many miles did the driver travel in total?
Sum the three distances: $$ 120 + 95 + 140 $$ $$ 120 + 140 = 260 $$ $$ 260 + 95 = 355 $$
Answer: 355 miles
Question 3
The decimal number five and forty-two hundredths is how much more than two and eighteen hundredths?
Write as decimals: \(5.42\) and \(2.18\).
Subtract: $$ 5.42 - 2.18 = 3.24 $$
Answer: 3.24

Question 6
Consider the first three odd numbers: 1, 3, 5.
a. What is their sum?
b. What is the square root of that sum?
Part a: \(1 + 3 + 5 = 9\)
Part b: The square root of 9 is 3 (because \(3 \times 3 = 9\)).
a. 9
b. 3
Question 7
The clock shows the time in the afternoon. What time was it 4 hours ago?
The clock shows 2:15.
If it is 2:15 PM, 4 hours ago would be:
2:00 PM -> 1:00 PM -> 12:00 PM -> 11:00 AM -> 10:00 AM.
Add the 15 minutes back: 10:15 AM.
Answer: 10:15 AM
Question 8
What type of angle is formed by the hands of this clock?
The clock shows 5:00. The angle spans 5 hour marks ($5 \times 30^\circ = 150^\circ$).
This is greater than 90 but less than 180.
Answer: Obtuse angle

Question 10
The height of a standard door is about 2 meters. About how many feet is that?
1 meter is a little more than 3 feet (approx 3.3 ft).

Answer: About 6 or 7 feet

Question 27
The radius of a circle is 10 inches. What is the diameter?
A. 5 in   B. 100 in   C. 20 in   D. 12 in
The diameter is always double the radius.
\(10 \times 2 = 20\) inches.
Answer: C (20 in)

Question 29
Find the 6th term of this sequence:
5, 10, 15, 20, ...

Question 30
Find the rule for this table:
Input: 3, 5, 10
Output: 9, 15, 30
\(3 \times 3 = 9\)
\(5 \times 3 = 15\)
The rule is multiplication.
Answer: Multiply Input by 3
