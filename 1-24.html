Question 1
Write and solve an equation:
A large bakery baked 583 loaves of bread in the morning and 649 loaves in the afternoon. How many loaves did they bake in total?
Add the morning amount to the afternoon amount: $$ 583 + 649 = 1232 $$
Answer: 1,232 loaves
Question 2
Sarah read 45 pages on Monday, 38 pages on Tuesday, and 52 pages on Wednesday. How many pages did she read in all three days?
Sum the pages from all days: $$ 45 + 38 = 83 $$ $$ 83 + 52 = 135 $$
Answer: 135 pages
Question 3
The decimal number four and sixty-five hundredths is how much more than one and twenty-five hundredths?
Translate to numbers: \(4.65\) and \(1.25\).
Subtract to find the difference: $$ 4.65 - 1.25 = 3.40 $$
Answer: 3.40 (or 3.4)
Question 4
a. Round 432 to the nearest hundred.
b. Round 432 to the nearest ten.
Part a: The tens digit is 3 (less than 5), so round down to 400.
Part b: The ones digit is 2 (less than 5), so round down to 430.
a. 400
b. 430
Question 5
Draw and shade circles to show the number \(1 \frac{3}{4}\).
Draw 2 circles. Shade the first one completely (representing 1). Divide the second into 4 parts and shade 3 of them.
Visual Answer: One full circle and one circle 3/4 shaded.
Question 6
The first four even numbers are 2, 4, 6, and 8.
a. What is their sum?
b. What is the square root of 36?
Part a: \(2 + 4 + 6 + 8 = 20\)
Part b: \(6 \times 6 = 36\), so \(\sqrt{36} = 6\).
a. 20
b. 6
Question 7
The clock shows the time right now. What time will it be in 3 hours?
The clock shows 8:20.
Add 3 hours: \(8 + 3 = 11\). The minutes stay the same.
Answer: 11:20
Question 8

Answer: Right angle
Question 9
a. Draw a segment labeled XY. Draw a segment labeled ZW that intersects XY.
b. Which segment is perpendicular to a vertical line?
Part a: Any two lines that cross each other (like an X or a +).
Part b: A horizontal line is perpendicular to a vertical line.
b. A horizontal segment
Question 10
Liam needs to cover a floor that is 10 yards long. About how many "BIG steps" will he take to walk the length?
A "BIG step" is approximately 1 yard (or 1 meter).

Question 26

b. What is its area?

Question 27
The diameter of a small circular rug is 4 feet. What is the radius?
A. 1 ft   B. 2 ft   C. 8 ft   D. 12 in
The radius is exactly half of the diameter.
\(4 \div 2 = 2\) feet.
Answer: B (2 ft)
Question 28
Put these money amounts in order from greatest to least:
3 quarters, 8 dimes, 15 nickels.
3 quarters: \(3 \times 0.25 = \$0.75\)
8 dimes: \(8 \times 0.10 = \$0.80\)
15 nickels: \(15 \times 0.05 = \$0.75\)
Order: 8 dimes is greatest. The others are equal.
Answer: 8 dimes, 3 quarters = 15 nickels
Question 29

7, 14, 21, 28, ...
These are multiples of 7.
8th term = \(7 \times 8\).
Answer: 56
Question 30
Find the rule for this table:
Input: 2, 4, 6, 8
Output: 10, 20, 30, 40
Look at the pairs: \(2 \to 10\), \(4 \to 20\).
\(2 \times 5 = 10\)
\(4 \times 5 = 20\)
The rule is multiply by 5.
Answer: Multiply Input by 5
