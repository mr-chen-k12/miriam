Problem 1
Keisha had a dime, two quarters, and six pennies. Write this amount with a dollar sign and decimal point.
Answer: $0.66

Explanation: First, determine the value of each coin:

  • 1 Dime = 10 cents ($0.10)
  • 2 Quarters = $25 \times 2 = 50$ cents ($0.50)
  • 6 Pennies = $1 \times 6 = 6$ cents ($0.06)

Add them together:

$$0.10 + 0.50 + 0.06 = 0.66$$

Written with a dollar sign: $0.66

Problem 2
Guymon is 328 miles west of Tulsa. Enid is 115 miles west of Tulsa. How far is it from Enid to Guymon?
Answer: 213 miles

Explanation:

  • Distance from Tulsa to Guymon = 328 miles.
  • Distance from Tulsa to Enid = 115 miles.

To find the distance between Enid and Guymon, we subtract the shorter distance from the longer distance:

$$328 - 115 = 213$$
Problem 3
Name the fraction or mixed number marked by the arrow on this number line:
Answer: \(2 \frac{3}{4}\)

Explanation:

Look at the space between the numbers (e.g., between 0 and 1). There are 3 tick marks between them, which divides the space into 4 equal sections. This means each mark represents \(\frac{1}{4}\).

The arrow is past the number 2. It is pointing to the 3rd mark after the 2.

So, the number is \(2\) and \(\frac{3}{4}\).

Problem 4
Compare:
\(12 + 24 + 45 \bigcirc 9 \times 9\)
Answer: =

Explanation: Solve both sides separately.

Left side: \(12 + 24 = 36\). Then, \(36 + 45 = 81\).

Right side: \(9 \times 9 = 81\).

Since \(81\) is equal to \(81\), the answer is =.

Problem 9
Which letter below has no right angles?
T L F W
Answer: W

Explanation:

  • T has right angles where the vertical and horizontal lines meet.
  • L has a right angle at the corner.
  • F has right angles where the horizontal lines meet the vertical line.
  • W is made of diagonal lines that form acute (sharp) angles, not right (90 degree) angles.
Problem 10
What is the perimeter of a rectangle that is 5 inches long and 3 inches wide?
Answer: 16 inches

Explanation: Perimeter is the distance around the shape.

$$Perimeter = (2 \times length) + (2 \times width)$$ $$P = (2 \times 5) + (2 \times 3)$$ $$P = 10 + 6 = 16$$

Or simply: \(5 + 5 + 3 + 3 = 16\).

Problem 11
Round 86 to the nearest ten. Round 84 to the nearest ten. Add the rounded numbers.
Answer: 170

Explanation:

  1. 86 rounded to the nearest ten is 90 (since 6 is 5 or greater, round up).
  2. 84 rounded to the nearest ten is 80 (since 4 is less than 5, round down).
  3. Add: \(90 + 80 = 170\).
Problem 12
If the diameter of a circle is two yards, then the radius is how many feet?
Answer: 3 feet

Explanation:

  1. The radius is half of the diameter. If diameter = 2 yards, then radius = 1 yard.
  2. Convert the radius to feet. Since 1 yard = 3 feet, the radius is 3 feet.

Problem 20
Find the missing number \(n\):
\( 48 + 61 + 28 + 32 + n = 208 \)
Answer: n = 39

Explanation:

Step 1: Add the known numbers together.

$$48 + 61 + 28 + 32$$
  • \(28 + 32 = 60\)
  • \(48 + 61 = 109\)
  • \(109 + 60 = 169\)

Step 2: Subtract that sum from the total to find \(n\).

$$208 - 169 = 39$$
